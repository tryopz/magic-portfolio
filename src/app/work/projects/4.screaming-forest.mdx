## Overview

Screamin Forest is a VR horror experience developed for Meta Quest 2. The game immerses players in a dark, atmospheric forest where survival depends on exploration, resourcefulness, and quick reflexes. Designed for maximum immersion, the project leverages Unity and C# to deliver interactive environments, dynamic audio, and intense gameplay moments.

- **Atmospheric Environments**: Dense forests, dynamic lighting, and spatial audio for heightened tension.
- **Survival Mechanics**: Resource management, exploration, and real-time threats.
- **Collaborative Development**: Built by a multidisciplinary team, combining technical and creative skills.

## Technologies Used

- **Unity**: Game engine for VR, rendering, and interaction.
- **C#**: Gameplay logic, environment systems, and mechanics.
- **Meta Quest SDK**: VR integration and device optimization.
